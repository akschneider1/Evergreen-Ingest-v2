Where this fits

  • One review pass. Two production-ready outputs. When a policy SME validates findings in Ingest, they produce two artifacts at once: a golden dataset of confirmed parameters for evaluation and monitoring, and source-grounded chunks — policy passages carrying their exact character positions and validation status — ready to embed and index into a vector store for RAG. The expert reviews once; both outputs are generated from the same session.
  • Ground truth for your AI evaluation pipeline. Each confirmed parameter is a ready-made test case: a known-correct value from the policy, a known-wrong value from the stale implementation, and the source sentence that proves it. Export to Evergreen Evals or push to a Langfuse dataset. Every eval failure traces back to the exact policy text that defines the correct answer.
  • Policy-grounded retrieval for the knowledge base. Validated parameters embed with their full lineage — domain, extraction class, character positions, and decision status — as vector metadata. Filter retrievals to decision == confirmed to serve only SME-verified content. Expand the context window at retrieval time using character positions to give the LLM the surrounding policy passage, not just the extracted sentence.
