Where this fits

  • Fix drift before it reaches the AI Assistant. The drift report shows exactly which content is stale or out of sync. Correct those passages in your source documents before they are added to the Knowledge Base — an AI Assistant will surface an outdated value just as confidently as the correct one. Verified parameters are stored alongside the policy text so the AI can answer with precision and cite its source.
  • Turn drift findings into automated answer checks. Every drifted or missing parameter gives you a known-correct value (from the policy) and a known-wrong value (from the stale implementation) — enough to build a concrete test. Evergreen Evals uses those findings to check that the AI Assistant answers with the policy value, not the outdated one. Run the checks after any Knowledge Base update to catch problems before they reach staff or citizens.
