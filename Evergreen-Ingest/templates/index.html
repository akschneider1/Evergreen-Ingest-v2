Where this fits

  • Review once, get two things. When you validate a finding, you produce two outputs at once: a confirmed fact that can be used to test AI systems, and a verified passage ready to add to a knowledge base. One session, two uses.
  • Every finding is traceable. Each confirmed difference includes the correct value from policy, the conflicting value from the implementation document, and the exact sentence that proves it. Export to Evergreen Evals to test AI systems, or use the JSON report to update training materials.
  • Your reviewed facts carry a full audit trail. Every confirmed finding records which document it came from, which passage, who reviewed it, and when. This makes it straightforward to update your knowledge base or training content — and to explain your decisions later.
